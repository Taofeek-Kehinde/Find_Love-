Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions we receive

How do I create an account on Find Love?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Find Love" button at the top right of any page, or go to our signup page. You'll need to provide some basic information and create a password. Once you verify your email address, you can start building your profile!

Is Find Love available internationally?

Yes! We have users in over 120 countries. Our platform is designed to help you connect with singles worldwide. You can filter matches by location, language, and cultural preferences to find your perfect international match.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our matching algorithm considers multiple factors including your profile information, preferences, personality traits, interests, and relationship goals. We use advanced compatibility scoring to suggest the most promising matches for you. The more complete your profile, the better our suggestions!

What security measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our top priority. We employ multiple security measures including profile verification, AI-powered scam detection, secure messaging, and the ability to report suspicious behavior. All personal information is encrypted and we never share your data with third parties.

How can I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go! You can delete your account by going to your account settings and selecting "Delete Account." Please note that this action is permanent and all your data will be removed from our system. If you're taking a break, you might prefer to temporarily deactivate your account instead.
