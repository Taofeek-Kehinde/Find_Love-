Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How does your matching algorithm work?

Our proprietary algorithm analyzes over 50 compatibility factors including personality traits, values, lifestyle preferences, and relationship goals. We combine psychological principles with machine learning to continuously improve match suggestions based on user feedback and success rates.

What makes your coaching different?

Our certified coaches specialize in modern dating challenges. Unlike generic advice, our coaching is personalized to your specific situation and goals. We focus on practical strategies for profile optimization, communication skills, and building authentic connections.

How often do you host events?

We host events weekly in major cities and monthly in smaller locations. Premium members get early access and discounts to all events. Our event calendar includes virtual speed dating, interest-based meetups, and exclusive destination weekends.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to additional features. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle. Pro-rated refunds are available for annual plans.

How do you ensure member safety?

Safety is our top priority. We employ multi-step verification, AI-powered behavior monitoring, 24/7 human moderation, and secure communication tools. All members must agree to our community guidelines, and we provide comprehensive safety resources and reporting tools.
