Our Love Story

Find Love was born from a simple idea, everyone deserves to find meaningful connections. Since 2020, we've been helping people around the world find love, friendship, and companionship through our innovative platform.

How It All Began

The journey of Find Love started with a personal experience

From Personal Experience to Global Mission

Our founder, Taofeek Kehinde, created Find Love after her own frustrating experiences with dating apps. he realized that most platforms focused on superficial connections rather than meaningful relationships.

In 2020, Kehinde gathered a team of relationship experts, psychologists, and tech enthusiasts to build a dating platform that would prioritize compatibility, safety, and genuine connections.

Today, Find Love has grown into a global community with millions of members in over 120 countries. We've helped create thousands of meaningful relationships and marriages, and we're just getting started.

At Find Love, we understand that love is one of the most powerful and beautiful parts of life — and everyone deserves to experience it. Our mission is to create a space where hearts can meet, connections can grow, and relationships can blossom into something meaningful and lasting. We’ve built Find Love to be more than just a dating platform. It’s a community of people from all walks of life who are ready to open their hearts, share their stories, and take a chance on something real. Whether you're looking for friendship, companionship, or the love of your life, Find Love is here to help you find that special person who understands you and values you for who you are. With an easy-to-use interface, secure messaging, and personalized matches, we’re committed to making your journey to love as smooth and rewarding as possible. We value honesty, respect, and authenticity — and we’ve created a safe space where meaningful relationships can thrive. Your story is unique, and your journey to love should be too. At Find Love, every message, every smile, and every connection is a step closer to something extraordinary. Join us and let love find you.
